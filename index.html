What is Fair Chance Hiring?

At minimum, Fair Chance Hiring means:

  • Hiring companies will not ask about an applicant’s criminal history or run any criminal background checks until after they’ve considered the candidate’s qualifications for a position.
  • Employers make inquiries about criminal histories only after they’ve decided a candidate is in the running for a job
  • Considering information about arrests not followed by convictions, participation in pretrial or post-trial diversion programs, or convictions that have been sealed, dismissed, expunged, or statutorily eradicated
